Elephant :

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their long trunks, large ears, and impressive tusks.

Key Facts

  • Species: African Elephant, Asian Elephant
  • Weight: Up to 12,000 pounds (5,400 kilograms)
  • Height: Up to 13 feet (4 meters)
  • Lifespan: 60-70 years
  • Habitat: Various types of habitats, including forests, savannas, and swamps

Image :

  • Elephants are highly intelligent and social animals, known for their strong family bonds and complex communication. Unfortunately, they are also endangered due to habitat loss and poaching for their ivory tusks.
